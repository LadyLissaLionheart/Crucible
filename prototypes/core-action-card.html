Dice Check
The Roll
4d12 vs Attribute Target Number (TN)

Roll four d12s. Each die that meets or beats your Attribute Target Number (TN) is a success. Each die that falls within the GM’s Risk zone (at or below the Risk number) is a penalty. Subtract penalties from successes to determine your result.

Even & Odd

Even dice favor you, odd dice favor the GM. The balance of even and odd dice determines both critical effects and resource generation.

Roll Effect Resources
4 even Critical Success Ignore penalties, +1 success; +2 Inspiration
3 even Critical Success +1 Inspiration
2 even / 2 odd Clash No resource gain
3 odd Critical Failure +1 Tension
4 odd Critical Failure Ignore successes, −1 success; +2 Tension
Inspiration
Gained from even dice
Clash
2 even / 2 odd — no gain
Tension
Gained from odd dice
Outcome

Your net successes (after subtracting penalties) determine the Success Ladder tier.

Net Successes Tier What Happens
−1 or less Misfortune Failure — things get complicated.
0 Setback Failure.
1 or more Triumph Clean success and beyond.
Timing
  • When you perform a core action or are asked to make a reaction.
Reminders
  • Spend Edges after the roll to shift one die up by 2 (one shift per Edge, die can only be shifted once).
  • Check parity after spending Edges to determine critical effects and resources.
  • On a Triumph, your Effect Magnitude equals the sum of your expertise dice (plus bonus successes if spent).
  • On a Setback, you may negotiate with the GM to apply magnitude at a cost.
  • Collect any Inspiration the roll generated — the GM collects Tension.
